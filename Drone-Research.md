## 2. SECTOR-WISE OPPORTUNITY ANALYSIS

### 2.1 🌾 AGRICULTURE (HIGHEST PRIORITY — 🔥🔥🔥)

**Market Opportunity: MASSIVE**

Bangladesh is an agriculture-dominant economy with:
- **8.83 million hectares** of cultivable land
- **12 million farm households**, 60 million smallholder farmers
- **Rice production**: 37.7+ million metric tons annually
- Only **~15%** of farmers have adopted precision agriculture hardware (drones included)
- Agricultural land declining ~1% annually → efficiency demand increasing

**Drone Applications:**
| Application | Demand Level | Pricing Potential |
|---|---|---|
| Pesticide Spraying | Very High | ৳500-1,500/acre |
| Fertilizer Spreading | High | ৳400-1,000/acre |
| Crop Health Monitoring (NDVI) | Medium-High | ৳300-800/acre |
| Seeding/Planting | Medium | ৳600-1,200/acre |
| Yield Estimation | Medium | ৳200-500/acre |
| Land Surveying/Mapping | High | ৳1,000-3,000/acre |

**Competitive Landscape:**
- **Sky Bees Ltd**: Only notable local manufacturer (founded 2024, targeting 7,000 drones/year from early 2025)
- Most farmers still use manual spraying (1 acre/day per worker)
- Drone spraying is **10-15x faster** than manual
- Government tenders exist via Department of Agricultural Extension

**Revenue Model:**
- Service-based: Charge per acre (৳500-1,500/acre)
- Seasonal: 3 spraying seasons per year (Boro, Aus, Aman rice)
- Potential: 1,000 acres/season = ৳50 lakh - ৳1.5 crore/season
- Fleet of 5 drones can cover 50-100 acres/day

## 4. REGULATORY FRAMEWORK

### 4.1 Governing Body
**CAAB** — Civil Aviation Authority of Bangladesh

### 4.2 Key Regulations
- **Drone Regulations 2024** (draft finalized, being implemented)
- **Air Navigation Order (ANO) Parts 947 & 945**
- Based on ICAO, EASA, and FAA standards

### 4.3 Drone Categories

| Category | Weight | Use Case | Requirements |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Open (A1)** | <250g | Recreational | No registration, no license |
| **Open (A2)** | 250g - 5kg | Recreational/Light | Registration, Open License (৳1,000) |
| **Specific** | 5kg - 25kg+ | Commercial | ROC, Specific License (৳2,000), CAAB authorization |
| **Certified** | Any (BVLOS, delivery) | Advanced commercial | Full certification, AOC |

### 4.4 License Costs
| License Type | Exam Fee (BDT) | Pass Mark | Age Min |
|---|---|---|---|
| Open (Private) | ৳1,000 | 75% | 16 years |
| Specific | ৳2,000 | 75% | 18 years |
| Certified | TBD | 75% | 18 years |

### 4.5 Key Requirements for Commercial Operations
1. Drone registration (≥250g)
2. Remote Pilot License (Specific category)
3. Remote Operator Certificate (ROC)
4. Insurance coverage
5. NOC from local authorities
6. CAAB operational authorization
7. Safety plan submission
8. Application **45 days in advance** for flight authorization

### 4.6 Prohibited Zones
- Military areas
- Airport vicinities
- Populated assemblies (without special permission)
- Government restricted zones

### 4.7 Penalties
- Fines
- Imprisonment (for security threats)
- License cancellation

## 6. IMPORT DUTY & SUPPLY CHAIN

### 6.1 Drone Import Considerations
- Bangladesh Customs classifies drones under specific HS codes
- Import duty varies: typically **5-28.73%** depending on classification
- VAT: **15%** standard
- AIT (Advance Income Tax): varies
- Total import cost can add **30-50%** to drone price

### 6.2 Key Import Sources
| Source | Pros | Cons |
|---|---|---|
| **China (DJI, etc.)** | Affordable, wide range | Import duty, shipping time |
| **Local (Sky Bees)** | No import duty, local support | Limited models, new company |
| **USA/Europe** | High quality | Very expensive, high duty |

### 6.3 Recommendation
- Start with **DJI drones** (industry standard, reliable)
- Partner with **Sky Bees** for local support/parts
- Explore **local assembly** in Year 2-3 to reduce costs

## 10. KEY SUCCESS FACTORS

1. **Regulatory compliance first** — Get all CAAB licenses before operations
2. **Start lean** — Service model, don't manufacture yet
3. **Build relationships** — Agriculture dealers, construction companies, film studios
4. **Invest in quality** — Better drones = better results = more clients
5. **Document everything** — Before/after footage, data reports, case studies
